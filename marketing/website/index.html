Scriptures

Study your scriptures without having to connect to the internet. Currently you can read either the King James Holy Bible or the LDS Standard Works without having to carry around the paper versions.

Currently the application has the following features:

  • Decreased database download times
  • Bookmarks
  • Screen orientation preference
  • Gesture navigation
  • Adjustible font size
  • Bread crumb navigation trail

But that's not all, we are working on getting more into the application, such as the following:

  • Highlighting, Notes
  • Search
  • More translations, including: American Standard Version (ASV), Spanish Reina Valera (Spanish RV), and Updated King James Version (UKJV) among others.
  • Additional books, including: Qua'ran, LDS Hymns, LDS Children's Songbook
