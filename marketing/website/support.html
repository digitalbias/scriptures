Support

There are a number of different options for support for the scripture application. In order of preference they are:

  1. The Google Group. Join the group and contribute to the discussion on features, bugs and direction for the application.
  2. The Blog. There is some useful information at the blog and the information there will be more up to date than this website, but it may take a while before your questions are answered. Longer than if you join the group anyway.
  3. Email Dave. Using the Android Market features, you could always "Email the Developer". Dave usually responds, but it may not be as fast as the first two options.

You can always donate.
